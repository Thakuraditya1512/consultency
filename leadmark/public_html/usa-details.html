Career Opportunities

The USA is one of the strongest economies in the world. In the United States, the highest earning occupational groups are white-collar professionals including managers, physicians, computer engineers, etc.

The USA has been at the forefront in telecoms, medicine, healthcare, and genetics. Studying in the USA for Indian students includes on-campus jobs as well. Students are allowed to work 20 hrs per week during the semester (on-campus).
